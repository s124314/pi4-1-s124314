English Translation

General Requirements

- The decree regulates the creation, development, commissioning, operation, decommissioning of state information systems, and storage of their data.

- Participants: government bodies, state corporations, private partners, concessionaires.

- Information security and personal data protection are mandatory.

System Creation

- Grounds: legal act, government decision, regional authority decision or concession agreement.

- A concept (with feasibility study) must be developed.

- Stages: documentation, software and working documentation, adjustment works, preliminary tests, pilot operation, acceptance tests.

- Iterative development is allowed.

Commissioning

- Formalized by a legal act of a government body.

- Requires: information security documents, system certification, staff training, intellectual property rights registration.

- Prohibited without a valid security certificate, registration of equipment in Russia, or proper IP rights.

System Development

- Conducted under the same rules as creation (except concept stage).

- Individual components can be tested and put into use before the entire system is completed.

Operation

- Performed strictly according to working documentation.

- Access to data must be provided in prescribed cases.

- Operation is prohibited if legal requirements for data and information protection are violated.

Decommissioning and Data Storage

- Grounds: end of service life, impracticality, or economic inefficiency.

- Formalized by a legal act.

- Includes: dismantling equipment, uninstalling software, archiving or destroying data, ensuring information security.

- Data must be stored for at least the same period as equivalent paper documents.
